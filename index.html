Northampton County Comittee shows support at Fourthof July Parade in Cape Charles
The Northampton County Democratic Committee and many of your fellow Eastern Shore Democrats marched in the Annual Cape Charles 4th of July parade to show their support for Senator Tim Kaine and Second Congressional District Democratic Candidate, Elaine Luria.

"New Look" Eastern Shore Democrats Newsletter

Welcome to the new Newsletter of the Eastern Shore Democrats, a cooperative service of Northampton County Virginia Democrats, and Accomack County Virginia Democrats. Please join us. We want you! We want your skills, your expertise, your passion, and your commitment to help us turn Virginia blue! To find out about Democratic meetings and events in your county, visit the Accomack County or the Northampton County web pages.
